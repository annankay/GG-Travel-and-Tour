Let's Explore Tunisia

Overview

Embark on a vibrant journey through Tunisia, where ancient history meets Mediterranean charm. This package offers an unforgettable experience, highlighting Tunisia’s diverse landscapes, ancient ruins, and pristine beaches. Perfect for history enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and beach lovers alike!

Package Inclusions

  • Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights
  • Accommodation: 4-star hotels with daily breakfast
  • Transportation: Comfortable air-conditioned buses for transfers and tours
  • Meals: Daily breakfast, 5 lunches, and 4 dinners
  • Guided Tours: English-speaking local guides
  • Entrance Fees: All major attraction fees included

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Tunis - Meet and greet, transfer to hotel, welcome dinner.
  • Day 2: Explore Tunis and Carthage - Guided tour of Tunis Medina and ancient ruins of Carthage.
  • Day 3: Kairouan - Day trip to Tunisia’s spiritual heart, explore the Great Mosque.
  • Day 4: Sahara Desert Adventure - Visit El Djem Amphitheater, camel ride into the dunes.
  • Day 5: Tozeur Oasis and Salt Flats - Jeep safari through desert landscapes.
  • Day 6: Hammamet Beach and Leisure - Relax on the Mediterranean coast.
  • Day 7: Departure from Tunis - Breakfast and leisure time before departure.
