About GG Capital Travel and Tours

GG Capital Travel and Tours is Ghana's foremost travel facilitator, offering unparalleled travel experiences to global visitors. We prioritize customer-centric, expertly managed services that surpass expectations.

Core Objective

To emerge as the premier tourism enterprise in Ghana and the surrounding region, distinguished by financial stability, customer focus, and exceptional management.

Strategic Vision

GG Capital Travel and Tours seeks to specialize in immersive, social, and educational travel experiences in Ghana through:

  1. Fostering tourism initiatives in educational institutions, workplaces, communities, and organizations.
  2. Promoting Ghana's tourism attractions globally.
  3. Ensuring seamless accommodation arrangements for domestic and international tourists.
  4. Providing comprehensive insurance solutions for travelers.

Business Philosophy

Our guiding principle revolves around delivering outstanding service, recognizing its pivotal role in customer acquisition and retention. We strive to innovate and stay ahead of industry trends.

Core Values

GG Capital Travel and Tours is renowned for:

  1. Responsiveness to client and partner needs.
  2. Uncompromising service quality and professionalism.
  3. Reliability, dependability, and integrity.
